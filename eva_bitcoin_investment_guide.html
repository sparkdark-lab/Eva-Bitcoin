Why Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)?
  • Reduces Risk: By investing the same amount regularly, you buy more when prices are low and less when prices are high
  • Removes Emotion: No need to guess the "perfect" time to invest‚Äîconsistency wins over timing
  • Builds Discipline: Creates a systematic approach to wealth building
  • Historical Success: DCA has proven effective across all major asset classes over long periods
